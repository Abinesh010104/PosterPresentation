About Us

The Department of Information Technology empowers students with the capability to apply knowledge of Information Technology, work efficiently in multidisciplinary teams, and practice engineering with ethical approaches. Our excellent lab facilities, constantly upgraded, provide ample opportunities for students to learn and innovate.

Students at our department leverage recent trends in Information Technology, including Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Blockchain Technology, Internet of Things, Social Networks, and Cyber Forensics. They develop innovative projects, addressing real-world problems in Agriculture, Health care, Environmental Science, Telecommunications, and Supply Chain sectors.

We take pride in our team of committed and dedicated faculty, our core strength in providing education with professional standards. Our faculty members guide students to be globally recognized as innovative computing professionals. We adopt improved methods of teaching and learning processes to enhance learning abilities through practice, exposure, and motivation.

The Department was granted the “Fund for Improvement of S&T Infrastructure (FIST)” by the Department of Science & Technology (DST). We boast good infrastructural and computing facilities, providing a conducive environment to promote academic and research excellence. We have successfully procured and executed funded research projects, further contributing to the growth and development of the department.

HOD Note

Welcome to the Department of Information Technology, Anna University, MIT Campus. As a well-known fact, we cannot imagine the world without information technology which has become an essential and inevitable part of our daily lives. The Department’s focus is to train our students to get strong academic knowledge in the recent trends of information technology like Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Internet of Things, Social Networks, Mobile Applications, Machine Learning, and Blockchain Technology, to make the students ready to face real-world challenges. The Department has a rich blend of young and highly-experienced regular and teaching-fellow faculty members, all holding Ph.D degree.

The department is active in organizing various workshops and seminars for the growth and development of faculty and students’ research knowledge. The department organizes International Conference ICRTIT (International Conference in the Recent Trends of Information Technology) to motivate research students and faculty to implement their innovative research ideas and to publish in the conference.

As a part of our encouragement to Innovation in Technology now we came up with an exciting event "POSTER PRESENTATION 2024" where we are open to hear your ideas and innovation and provide seeding feedbacks from domain experts and small encouragements to your innovative ideas. Excel with us!!
